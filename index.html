DIY Recipe for Hydrated Hair

Here's a simple recipe to hydrate your kinky hair:

  1. Mix one tablespoon of coconut oil with one tablespoon of honey.
  2. Apply this mixture to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes.
  3. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  4. Repeat once a week for optimal results.
